ESTP vs ESFP: Key Differences

Comparison guide · ESTP (The Entrepreneur) vs ESFP (The Entertainer) · By Brad Kam

The core difference

The key difference between ESTP and ESFP is the T/F axis. ESTPs decide by logic first; ESFPs decide by values first.

ESTP (The Entrepreneur) and ESFP (The Entertainer) share 3 of 4 MBTI letters. The single-axis flip on T/F produces a noticeably different daily rhythm.

Where they overlap

ESTPs and ESFPs share 3 core preferences. Both tend to gravitate toward similar environments, similar work, and similar people. To outsiders they can look nearly identical — which is why they're commonly confused.

Where they diverge most

Primary divergence: T/F. ESTPs decide by logic first; ESFPs decide by values first. This shows up in leadership style, conflict handling, and how they recover from stress.

How to tell which one you are

The single flip (T/F) is the decisive tell. Ask yourself: When you disagree with someone close to you, does the logic of their argument (T) or the feelings in the room (F) come up first for you?
