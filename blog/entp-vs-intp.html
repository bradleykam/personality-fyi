The core difference

ENTPs recharges with people and thinks out loud; INTPs recharges alone and processes internally before speaking.

ENTP (The Debater) and INTP (The Logician) share 3 of 4 MBTI letters. They are commonly mistaken for each other.

ENTP vs INTP: at a glance

  • Energy / I-E: ENTP extroverts and recharges with people; INTP introverts and recharges alone.
  • Information / N-S: ENTP leads with abstraction and patterns; INTP leads with abstraction and patterns.
  • Decisions / T-F: ENTP decides on logic first; INTP decides on logic first.
  • Lifestyle / J-P: ENTP keeps options open; INTP keeps options open.
  • Population: ENTP is roughly 3-5%; INTP is roughly 3-5%.

Where they overlap

ENTP and INTP share 3 core preferences. They tend to gravitate toward similar environments, similar work, and similar people. To outsiders they can look nearly identical — which is why they are commonly confused.

Where they diverge most

Primary divergence: I/E. ENTPs recharges with people and thinks out loud; INTPs recharges alone and processes internally before speaking. This shows up in leadership style, how they handle conflict, and how they recover from stress.

How to tell which one you are

The decisive tell is the I/E flip. Ask yourself: After a big group event, do you feel recharged (E) or drained (I)?

When in doubt, take the 60-second test — it scores word patterns and preference signals rather than abstract self-description.
