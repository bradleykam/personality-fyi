Contact

Brief is better than long

Email

Best way to reach us: hello@personality.fyi.

Response within 72 hours on most weekdays. Include enough context that a stranger could act on your message without follow-ups.

What to send

  • Corrections: if a type description, famous example, or career list has an error, tell us what’s wrong and why.
  • Feature requests: describe the use case, not just the feature. We’re more likely to build something if we understand the underlying need.
  • Partnership / content: include a specific idea and a link to prior work.
  • Bug reports: what you did, what happened, what you expected, and which browser.
  • Data deletion requests: email from the address on file; we’ll delete within 7 days.

What we don’t respond to

Cold outreach about SEO services, growth hacks, or AI SaaS that we didn’t ask about. Also: any email that starts with "Dear website owner."
